The Problem

The Urgency of Tackling Food Waste

There exists a huge gap between the surplus food as leftovers at social events and the number of people going to bed with empty stomachs.

Resource depletion.
The production of food requires significant resources such as land and water. When this food is wasted, these resources are wasted, resulting in a depletion of valuable natural resources.
Health hazards.
Food waste generates harmful gases, bacteria, and chemicals that can be harmful to human health, especially for those who work in waste management facilities or live near landfills.
Food insecurity.
Exacerbates the issue of food insecurity, where a significant portion of the population lacks access to adequate, nutritious food.
Economic losses.
Food waste has significant economic implications, including costs associated with production, transportation, and disposal.

Our Solution

Bridging the gap

Our solution is aimed at bridging the gap between surplus food sources and people in need, with a specific focus on reducing food waste from events like weddings and banquets.

Surplus Food Donation.
Our web portal allows for easy surplus food donation from caterers and banquet halls, through a process so simple that everyone can hop onto.
Verification and Distribution.
The food will be distributed to NGOs and orphanages upon verification, making sure that it reaches those in need.
Integration with Google Maps API.
With the integrated Maps API, the food is transported in a timely and efficient manner, reducing the time it takes to get the food to those in need.
